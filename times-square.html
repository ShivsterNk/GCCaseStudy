To get from Grand Central - LIRR Platform, to Times Square (Staircase Area)

By Walking

  1. Exit from the platform your LIRR train arrives on and head to the mezzanine. Then, take the 47th Street escalators to the concourse.
  2. Once there, find the exit for 47th Street, where you will end up on street level near 47th Street and Madison Avenue.
  3. Do note, there are 2 staircases at this area, separated by the ticket machine.

  4. Walk South towards 46th Street. Then make a RIGHT at 46th Street and Madison Avenue, and walk WEST along 46th Street until you reach 7th Avenue.
  5. Once you reach 46th Street and 7th Avenue, you will see the red staircase at Duffy's Square.

Some Tips and Tricks

  1. At Grand Central, there is a set of elevatorsthat will take you up from the mezzanine to the concourse.
  2. It's located between the 46th Street and 47th Street escalator banks on the mezzanine, so look for signs.
  3. There is another elevator available on the Concourse level, that will take you to the Street level.

  4. This elevator is located in a small corner past the ticket at 47th Street, facing away from the 47th Street escalator bank.
  5. Another option, is to directly walk WEST on 47th Street, from Madison Avenue to 7th Avenue.
  6. At 7th Avenue, cross the street, and you will see the back of the staircase. Walk towards it.

Expected Time Taken - Minutes

  1. To get onto street level from the LIRR tracks, it will take you roughly 5 Minutes depending on your speed and method you chose.
  2. To get from the street level (near 47th and Madison) to the Red Staircase at Times Square, it will take roughly 11 Minutes, depending on your speed.
