To get to Central Park - Bethseda Terrace

NOTE: The fastest way to get there is by bus. Even if its a beautiful day outside, the bus will save you time and energy.

There are two different ways to get out of Grand Central, and two different locations to board any of the buses from.

Regardless of which way you chose, make sure to take the bus and get off at Madison Avenue and 72nd Street.

Click a choice to show how to get the bus from the LIRR platforms.

Please view both, as one may better suit your needs.

To Get The Bus - OPTION 1

    Exit from the platform your LIRR train arrives on to the mezzanine. Then, head up the 47th Street escalators to the concourse.

    Then, exit onto street level where you'll land at 47th Street and Madison Avenue.

    Walk southbound to 46th Street and Madison Avenue

    There are 2 places you can board the bus:

    Near East 46th Street and Madison - Northbound M1 or M2 Bus

    Near East 45th Street and Madison - Northbound M3 or M4 Bus

To Get The Bus - OPTION 2

    Exit from the platform your LIRR train arrives on to the mezzanine. Then, head up the 48th Street escalators to the concourse.

    Then, take the elevator to street level, where you'll exit near the corner of 48th Street and Madison Avenue.

    Walk northbound to 49th Street and Madison Avenue

    There are 2 places you can board the bus:

    Near East 49th Street and Madison - Northbound M3 or M4 Bus

    Near East 50th Street and Madison - Northbound M1 or M2 Bus

Here's the buses to look out for + Directions:

Board an M1 Bus going to Harlem-147th Street
Board an M2 Bus going to Washington Heights
Board an M3 Bus going to Fort George
Board an M4 Bus going to the Cloisters
❮ ❯

    Regardless of which stop you board at, or which bus you take...

    You MUST get off at East 72nd Street and Madison Avenue!!

    After getting off, walk towards 72nd Street and then turn to walk towards 5th Avenue.

    Then walk straight into Central Park via the Terrace Avenue walkway entrance.

    Continue to walk via Terrace Avenue, where you will reach the Bethseda Terrace!

Some Tips and Tricks

  1. At Grand Central, there is a set of elevatorsthat will take you up from the mezzanine to the concourse.
  2. It's located between the 46th Street and 47th Street escalator banks on the mezzanine, so look for signs.
  3. There is another elevator available on the Concourse level, that will take you to the Street level.

  4. This elevator is located in a small corner past a few ticket machines, facing away from the 47th Street escalator bank.
  5. Note: During all daytimes, the M2 Bus operates Express. The only stop you can take this bus from is at 50th Street and Madison Avenue.
  6. Note: Some M4 Buses operate Express during weekday rush hours. The only stop you can take this bus from is at 49th Street and Madison Avenue.

Expected Time Taken - Minutes

  1. To get onto street level from the LIRR tracks, it will take you roughly 5-7 minutes depending on your speed.
  2. To get the bus and ride from either 45-46 Streets or 49-50th Streets, to Madison and 72nd Street, should take 12-17 minutes
  3. This depends on which bus stop you board from, which bus comes your way, and traffic.
  4. To get from The 72nd Street Bus Stop to Bethseda Terrace via 72nd Street and Terrace Avenue, should take 11 minutes, depending on your speed.
