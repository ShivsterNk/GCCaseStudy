To get from Grand Central - LIRR Platform, to Bryant Park

By Walking

  1. Exit from the platform your LIRR train arrives on and head to the mezzanine. Then, take the 45th Street escalators to the concourse.

  2. Once there, walk to the 44th Street area. Then, turn right.
  3. Here, you will find signs to 2 elevators. Take the elevator that says Elevator to 44th St to the right.

  4. Once on street level, turn right and Walk West along 44th Street to 5th Avenue.
  5. Then make a LEFT at 44th Street and 5th Avenue, and walk SOUTH along 5th Avenue until you reach 42nd Street.
  6. Once you reach 42nd Street and 5th Avenue, walk along 42nd Street, where you will see Bryant Park.

Some Tips and Tricks

  1. At Grand Central, there is a set of elevatorsthat will take you up from the mezzanine to the concourse.
  2. It's located between the 46th Street and 47th Street escalator banks on the mezzanine, so look for signs.

Expected Time Taken - Minutes

  1. To get onto street level from the LIRR tracks, it will take you roughly 6-7 minutes depending on your speed and method you chose.
  2. To get from the street level (near 44th and Madison) to Bryant Park, it will take roughly 8 minutes, depending on your speed.
