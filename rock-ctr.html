To get to Rockefeller Center from Grand Central LIRR

By Walking

  1. Exit from the platform your LIRR train arrives on and head to the mezzanine.
  2. For exiting the mezzanine to the street, you have two choices.
  3. Choice 1: Take the 47th Street escalator to the concourse. Then exit to street by the stairs or elevator.
  4. Choice 2: Take the 48th Street escalator to the concourse, then walk straight. Take the elevator that will bring you to the street level.

  5. Once at street level, from either exit, walk NORTH along Madison Avenue, until you reach 49th Street.
  6. Then turn LEFT and walk along 49th Street, passing 5th Avenue, where then you will arrive at Rockefeller Center.

Some Tips and Tricks

  1. At Grand Central, there is a set of elevatorsthat will take you up from the mezzanine to the concourse.
  2. It's located between the 46th Street and 47th Street escalator banks on the mezzanine, so look for signs.
  3. When you exit the 48th Street elevator, it will leave you in the middle of the sidewalk.

  4. From it, walk west and cross Madison Avenue, then turn right to continue your trip to 49th and beyond.
  5. The faster way to get to Rockefeller Center, is by using the 48th Street escalator bank, and its connected elevator to the street.
  6. To make this faster, make sure you board towards the back of the train going to Grand Central.

Expeccted Time Taken - Minutes

  1. To get onto street level from the LIRR tracks, it will take roughly 5 Minutes depending on your speed.
  2. To get from the street level (near 47th/48th Streets and Madison Avenue) to Rockefeller Center, it will take roughly 7 Minutes, depending on your speed.
